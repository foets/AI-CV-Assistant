## HOW IT WORKS

You provide your experience ONCE here. For each job application:
- Paste the job URL or description into chat
- The agent analyzes job requirements
- Creates a tailored CV emphasizing relevant parts of YOUR experience
- Uses the job's exact terminology for skills you have
- Generates a PDF ready for submission

**The agent will NOT invent experience** - it only tailors what you provide.

## WHAT TO INCLUDE

**Be comprehensive** - include ALL your experience, skills, and achievements. The agent will select what's relevant for each job.

**Include metrics** - numbers make CVs powerful:
- Revenue/cost impact: "$200M portfolio", "reduced costs by 30%"
- Efficiency gains: "2× faster", "cut processing time by 60%"
- Scale: "40+ countries", "team of 15"

**List all skills/tools you know** - even basic ones. The agent matches these to job requirements.
